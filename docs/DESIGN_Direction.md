# Daily Essentials 🌿

### Product Design & Brand Direction

---

# 1. Product Vision

Daily Essentials is not a cannabis marketplace, delivery service, or e-commerce product.

It is a friend-group coordination platform.

The goal is to make planning casual hangouts effortless.

Users can:

* Plan meetups
* Manage supplies
* Split responsibilities
* Chat together
* Customize their group experience
* Share live location (future feature)

The experience should feel like a combination of:

* Discord
* WhatsApp
* Snapchat
* Duolingo's personality
* Modern gaming UI

The app should never feel corporate.

The product should feel alive.

---

# 2. Emotional Design Goals

Users should feel:

### Welcome

The app should feel immediately friendly.

No sterile enterprise UI.

No dashboard feeling.

---

### Playful

Everything should feel enjoyable.

Small animations.

Mascots.

Micro-interactions.

Tiny moments of delight.

---

### Social

The design should emphasize people.

Avatars.

Friend status.

Group activity.

Presence.

Everything should reinforce community.

---

### Cozy

The visual atmosphere should feel like:

Late evening.

Good friends.

Relaxed vibes.

Nature.

Warm conversations.

---

# 3. Visual Identity

The entire brand is inspired by:

* Nature
* Leaves
* Organic shapes
* Friend groups
* Campfire energy
* Modern gaming interfaces

---

# 4. Design Language

Avoid:

❌ Sharp edges

❌ Enterprise layouts

❌ Corporate SaaS dashboards

❌ Banking app aesthetics

❌ Excessive data density

❌ Tiny UI elements

---

Prefer:

✅ Large touch targets

✅ Rounded shapes

✅ Soft shadows

✅ Organic spacing

✅ Glassmorphism

✅ Playful animations

✅ Chunky buttons

✅ Friendly typography

---

# 23. Final Design Summary

Build something that feels like:

"Discord meets WhatsApp meets Duolingo in a cozy nighttime forest."

The user should immediately think:

"This is where my friend group hangs out."

Not:

"This is another productivity app."

The product should feel alive, social, playful, and memorable.
